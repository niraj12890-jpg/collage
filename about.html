About Kisan Inter College

Kisan Inter College provides quality education up to 12th grade under UP Madhymik Shiksha Parishad. Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, skill, and confidence for their future.

We have well-experienced faculty, modern classrooms, labs, and library facilities to ensure the best academic environment for students.
